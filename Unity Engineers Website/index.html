About Us

Unity Engineers Construction Company

Established in 2015, Unity Engineers has been a leading name in the construction industry in Lahore, Pakistan. Under the supervision of our experienced partners Mr. Abdul Majid and Mirza Muhammad Imran Baig, we have successfully completed numerous residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

Our company has vast experience in commercial multi-stories buildings, residential towers, industrial buildings, and housing schemes. We pride ourselves on delivering quality construction with attention to detail and timely completion.

Registered under the Partnership Act, 1932, Unity Engineers is committed to excellence in every project we undertake.
